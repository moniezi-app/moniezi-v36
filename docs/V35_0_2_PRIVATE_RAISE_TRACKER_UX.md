# MONIEZI v35.0.2 — Private Raise Tracker UX

This release keeps the v35 Pro Finance fundraising feature set and simplifies the owner-facing Equity area for small business owners and early startup founders.

## Changes

- Renamed the Equity screen from **Company Equity Register** to **Private Raise Tracker**.
- Replaced the dense corporate subtitle with a short plain-English owner workflow.
- Reorganized navigation around the business-owner questions:
  - Interest
  - Packages
  - Funding
  - SAFEs / Agreements
  - Issued Ownership
  - Ownership Summary
  - People & Entities
  - Settings
- Added a new **Funding Status** panel showing interested/pledged, signed packages, active agreements, and recorded funded shares.
- Changed the heavy warning block into a smaller expandable internal-records note.
- Renamed several headings to reduce legal/cap-table jargon.
- Fixed the top settings button behavior by routing it through the same section-opening flow as the rest of the screen.

## Product role

MONIEZI Pro Finance remains the private owner/admin tool for tracking business finance and internal raise records. MONIEZI Raise remains the separate investor-facing signup and package page.
