# MONIEZI v32.1.0 — Equity Module Guide

This version expands the internal Company Equity Register with clearer demo data, a guided share issuance workflow, and an internal investment reservation tracker.

## Use order

1. Equity Settings: confirm company name, state, authorized shares, par value, and share classes.
2. Stakeholders: add the person or entity first.
3. Issue Shares: record the actual share issuance transaction.
4. Cap Table: review ownership percentages calculated from outstanding issued shares.
5. Reservations: track investor indications of interest that are not yet issued shares.
6. SAFEs: track convertible instruments separately until they convert into shares.

## Important distinction

Stakeholders are contacts/entities. They are not necessarily shareholders.
A stakeholder becomes a shareholder only when an issued-share record is created in the Share Issuance Ledger.

Reservations and SAFEs are not counted in the cap table until converted into issued shares.
